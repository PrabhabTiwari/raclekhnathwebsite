About Us

Who We Are

The Rotaract Club of Lekhnath is a service club for young men and women ages 18 to 30 who are dedicated to community and international service. We are part of Rotary International, one of the world's largest service organizations.

Our club provides an opportunity for young adults to enhance their knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and to promote better relations between all people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service.

Community Service

We organize various community service projects to improve the quality of life in our local area.

International Service

We participate in international projects and collaborate with Rotaract clubs worldwide.

Professional Development

We provide opportunities for leadership development and professional networking.
