About Rotary Club of Lekhnath

The Rotary Club of Lekhnath is a dedicated group of professionals, community leaders, and visionaries committed to making a lasting impact. Serving as the proud parent and sponsor club for the Rotaract Club of Lekhnath and its child clubs, we provide mentorship, guidance, and unwavering support to empower the next generation of leaders. Together, we work towards sustainable community development, international fellowship, and the core principles of Rotary International.

Message from President

Guiding our community with vision and dedication

Rtn. Shiva Raj Pandit

President, Rotary Club of Lekhnath

It is my distinct honor to extend warmest greetings on behalf of the Rotary Club of Lekhnath. As the parent club, we are immensely proud of the outstanding achievements and the dynamic leadership demonstrated by the Rotaract Club of Lekhnath and its child clubs.

Rotary has always been about fellowship, integrity, and impactful service. It brings us great joy to see these core values being upheld and amplified by our young leaders. The dedication you show in community service and professional development gives us confidence in a brighter future.

Our club remains fully committed to supporting the Rotaract movement in the Lekhnath region. We believe in providing the mentorship and resources necessary to empower the youth. The bridge between Rotary and Rotaract is one of mutual respect and collaborative spirit, and we will continue to foster this strong connection.

As you step into another Rotary year filled with new opportunities, I encourage you to keep pushing the boundaries of what you can achieve. Embrace the theme of this year, stay true to the Rotary Four-Way Test, and let your passion for service inspire others.

Together, we can create a lasting legacy of positive change in our communities and across the globe. We stand beside you as partners in service.

Jay Rotary!! Jay Rotaract!!

Rtn. Shiva Raj Pandit

President
