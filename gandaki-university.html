About Rotaract Club of Gandaki University

The Rotaract Club of Gandaki University is a vibrant community of young leaders and students dedicated to creating a positive impact. Operating as a child club under the Rotaract Club of Lekhnath, we bring together passionate individuals from the university to engage in meaningful community service, professional development, and international fellowship.

Message from Charter President

Leading with vision and dedication at Gandaki University

Rtr. Prabhab Tiwari

Charter President

It gives me immense pride and happiness to extend my heartfelt congratulations to the President and Board of Directors of the Rotaract Club of Lekhnath for Rotary Year 2026–27. I wish you a successful tenure filled with meaningful service, inspiring leadership, and remarkable achievements.

The establishment of our club was made possible through the unwavering support, mentorship, and guidance of our sponsoring club, the Rotaract Club of Lekhnath. Their encouragement and commitment have been instrumental in laying a strong foundation for our growth, and for that, we remain sincerely grateful.

The relationship between the Rotaract Club of Gandaki University and the Rotaract Club of Lekhnath is built upon the shared values of fellowship, service, leadership, and mutual respect. I firmly believe that this bond will continue to strengthen through collaborative projects, leadership initiatives, and opportunities that empower young people to create meaningful and lasting impact in their communities.

As the Charter President, it is truly fulfilling to witness the club progressing with the same passion and vision with which it was founded. Every new leadership team carries forward the responsibility of preserving our values while introducing fresh ideas and innovative approaches to service. I have complete confidence that the current leadership will take the club to even greater heights.

I extend my best wishes to all members as you embark on another remarkable Rotary year. May your journey be filled with learning, friendship, and opportunities to make a positive difference both within and beyond the university. Let us continue to uphold the spirit of Service Above Self and work together to build a stronger, more impactful Rotaract movement.

Together, let us strengthen our collaboration, inspire future leaders, and create a legacy of service that will benefit generations to come.

Jay Rotary!! Jay Rotaract!!

Rtr. Prabhab Tiwari

Charter President
