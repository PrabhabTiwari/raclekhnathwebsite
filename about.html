Our History

The Rotaract Club of Lekhnath has emerged as a dynamic force for positive change, uniting passionate young leaders to address critical community needs in Nepal's scenic "Garden City of Seven Lakes." Through impactful initiatives ranging from health camps and educational support to environmental conservation and leadership development programs, the club has made significant strides in improving lives while nurturing the next generation of socially responsible leaders. By fostering strong partnerships with local organizations and Rotary International, the club has amplified its reach, creating sustainable solutions that blend compassion with action. Their dedication to service above self has not only transformed communities but also inspired a culture of youth empowerment, proving that meaningful change begins with determined individuals working together toward a common vision of progress and unity.

The Four-Way Test

Our ethical guide for everything we think, say or do

1

Is it the TRUTH?

We uphold honesty and integrity in all our actions and communications.

2

Is it FAIR to all concerned?

We ensure equity and justice in all our projects and decisions.

3

Will it build GOODWILL and BETTER FRIENDSHIPS?

We foster positive relationships within our club and with the community.

4

Will it be BENEFICIAL to all concerned?

We ensure our projects create meaningful impact for all stakeholders.

Our Mission & Values

Guiding principles that drive our actions and decisions

Service

We believe in putting community needs first through meaningful service projects that create lasting impact in areas like education, health, and environment.

Fellowship

Building genuine connections among members to create a supportive network of young leaders through regular meetings and social events.

Innovation

Developing creative solutions to address community challenges in sustainable ways, adapting to changing needs and technologies.

Leadership

Empowering members with skills and opportunities to become ethical, effective leaders through training and hands-on project management.

Our Rotary Connection

As part of the Rotary International family (District 3292, Nepal-Bhutan), we benefit from a global network of service-minded individuals and organizations. Our club is proudly sponsored by the Rotary Club of Pokhara Annapurna, which provides mentorship, guidance, and support for our initiatives.

Through this connection, we participate in international service projects, youth exchanges, and global grant opportunities that amplify our local impact. We also collaborate with our sponsor club on joint initiatives that serve the Lekhnath community.
